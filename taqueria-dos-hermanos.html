Taqueria Dos Hermanos

Wireframe Redesign

Project Description

  This project was a wireframe redesign for a website of our choosing for INFO 110 (Introduction to Human-Computer Interaction). In groups, we were to select a website that we felt could be changed in ways that would better follow design principles and guidelines; then, each group member would design new wireframes for aspects of that website's pages accordingly.

  Our group chose to redesign wireframes for the Squarespace website for Philadelphia-based food truck Taquería Dos Hermanos. Each group member was tasked with designing new wireframes for a different page of the website, of which I redesigned the home page. We then presented our changes to the class explaining our rationale.

Note: At the time of writing, Taquería Dos Hermanos has a newer website here.
